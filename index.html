The benefits of moving to Portugal for Canadians:

CLIMATE

Portugal has a warm Mediterranean climate with long, sunny summers and mild winters. This makes it a great place to live if you're looking for a change from Canada's colder climate.

CULTURE

Portugal has a rich and vibrant culture, with a history that dates back thousands of years. There are plenty of museums, festivals, and cultural events to explore, as well as delicious local cuisine.

COST OF LIVING

Portugal is known for having a relatively low cost of living in Western Europe.
This can make it easier for Canadians to stretch their budget and enjoy a comfortable lifestyle.

QUALITY OF LIFE

Portugal is consistently ranked as one of the top countries in the world for quality of life. With its relaxed pace of life, beautiful scenery, and friendly locals, it's easy to see why.
